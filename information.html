Trinity College Chapel

6 Hoskin Ave, Toronto, Ontario

Ceremony begins at 11:00 AM

Details

The ceremony will begin promptly at 11:00 AM. We kindly ask that guests arrive early and be seated by 10:45 AM to ensure a timely and uninterrupted start.

Please note that the chapel does not permit the throwing of confetti, rice, flower petals, or birdseed at any time.

Add to Calendar (iCal for Google, Apple, Outlook) .

Directions & Parking

By Car
Limited street parking may be available nearby, and there are several paid parking garages in the surrounding area. We recommend parking at 107 St George Street (Information | Google Maps | Apple Maps).

By Transit
The chapel is easily accessible via the TTC. The closest subway station is Museum Station on Line 1, just a short walk from the chapel. St. George Station is another nearby option.

Walking
From the main streets, make your way toward Hoskin Avenue and follow signage for Trinity College within the University of Toronto campus.

Oretta King West

633 King St W, Toronto, Ontario

Festivities begin at 2:00 PM

Details

We’re excited to continue the celebration at Oretta, in Toronto’s King West neighbourhood.

The reception will begin at 2:00 PM and will include a cocktail hour, followed by a late lunch and dessert. The celebration will conclude at 6:00 PM. We look forward to celebrating, dining, and spending a joyful afternoon together!

Add to Calendar (Google, Apple, Outlook)

Directions & Parking

By Car
Oretta is located on King Street West, near Bathurst Street. A public underground parking garage is available just east of the venue at 621 King Street West - Lot #505. Additional parking is available in a garage attached to 650 King Street West - Lot #504, adjacent to the venue.

By Transit
The venue is accessible via the TTC. The King streetcar runs along King Street West and stops within a short walk of the venue.
